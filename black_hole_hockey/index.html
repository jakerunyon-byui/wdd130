Our program has a proven track record to produce winners. Our goal goes far beyond producing good hockey players. We want to produce productive, disciplined and young men and women who can be pillars of positive influence in their community and families. Our coaching network stretches across the country and whether your goal is to get play high level youth hockey, college hockey or make the pros, we will put you in the best position to do it.
People outside of Las Vegas don't understand the strengh of our community. The Golden Knights made their debut just days after the One October Mass Shooting that crippled our city. Hockey has become a rallying point in this city. A lot of people didn't think that hockey would succeed in Sin City. Those people fail to realize that there is so much more to Las Vegas beyong The Strip. Black Hole Hockey Club wants to serve a gathering place for our beloved 702.
Goaltending is equal parts art, science, and chaos. The Black Hole Hockey Club believes that we have unlocked a method to madness to developing goalies. By utilizing cutting-edge technology like augmented reality and virtual reality prospective goaltenders can practice all facets of the game even if they aren't on the ice. Auston Matthews has become the league's best scorer and he come from Phoenix, AZ. Who says that a future Vezina winner can't come from Las Vegas?
