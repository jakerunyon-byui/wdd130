Bambis

This clinic in tailored for newcomers to the sport. The class will focus primarily on skating, with some instruction on basic gameplay and hockey strategy. Participating will include a complimentary jersey and t-shirt. Full gear, helmets, and sticks will not be provided. Skates are available in the rental shop. Dates: Monday - Friday. Times: 4:00-5:15 Rates: $20

Mites

After passing a skating proficiency exam, our Bambis can move up to the mites clinic This clinic is catered towards beginners who making the jump to more competitive level. Skating instruction is more focused on power skating, and edge work. Stickhandling, shooting and passing is another emphasis. Dates: Monday – Friday. Times: 5:30-6:45 rates: $20

Astronauts

This special invite only clinic is meant to develop players to a college level skill or higher. These clinics include detailed skating technique for specific game situations. Drills will include 1 on 1 puck battles, 3 on 2 odd rushes, and forecheck strategy. Advanced clinic attendees are invited to try out for the traveling club teams (14 & under, 16 & under, 18 & under). Dates: Monday – Saturday, Times: 7:00-9:00. Rates: $20, free for members of club teams.
